Welcome to the
Chocolate Revolution

We’ve all seen the meteoric rise of coffee - but what about those of us who seek luxurious, rich, velvety drinks that hail from all over the world?

Look no further than Culture Choc!

Our mission is to bring the wonders of the world (in chocolate form) to street food goers the world over... although we will settle for Glasgow and the surrounding areas for now.

Whether you crave a simple delight in the form of Belgian or French cuisine, the spice of Mexico and Hungary, or even the uniqueness of our own blend of India and Ruby (yes, we have Ruby hot chocolate!), there is something for everyone.

We will be updating our market stall presence below and on our socials, so say hi when you come to visit!

Our menu rotates with each market but you can find all our flavours in our Menu above - and if a particular flavour inspires you, message us on Instagram and it may just feature at the next market you visit!

Finally, if you have a wedding, get together or big corporate event you'd like us to cater, drop us a note on our Private Hire page!

We hope to see you soon and until then - keep choc-ing along.

Why Culture Choc?

Alex has a passion for creating delicious businesses. His background is in IT project management but he has always dreamed of bringing something delicious to street food festivals.

While attending many (many!) street food markets, he realised that apart from coffee, there weren’t many drink options for those who may not want caffeine or have the ability to tolerate it. After many hours of testing and refining, Culture Choc was born!
